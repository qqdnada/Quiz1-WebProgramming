Local Food

Soto Banjar

Main ingedient of Soto Banjar is chicken. Soto Banjar has a fragrant aroma of spices such as cinnamon, nutmeg and cloves. The sauce is mixed with a little milk which makes the color of the soup not clear, but a little cloudy.

This soup contains shredded chicken, with additional perkedel, boiled potatoes, boiled eggs, carrots and ketupat.

Nasi Kuning Banjar

This nasi kuning is different with nasi kuning in Java. Nasi kuning Banjar is served with side dishes in the form of ayam masak habang made from red chilies as well as several other additions such as mie tongseng and a mix of fried onions.

Nasi kuning Banjar doesn't use the additon of Jeruk Purut leaves. The rice used is also also original Banjar rice, namely Siamese rice.
